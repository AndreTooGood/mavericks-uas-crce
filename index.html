About Us

We are the Mavericks-UAS team of FRCRCE (Bandra West). We are a technical team that works on the development of drones and other aerial vehicles. The team focuses on creating UAV's that are better, cost effective and efficient than the other UAV's available in the market. We take part in different national and international competitions to test our abilities and showcase our interests. Our motto is 'To do the best we can do.' With each competition that we take part in, we try to bring in something new and groundbreaking into the world of aerial flight systems. There is genuine understanding as well as cooperation within the team and at the same time a sense of responsibility.
