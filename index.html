ABOUT US

We are the Mavericks-UAS team of CRCE (Bandra West). We are a technical team that works on the development of drones and other aerial vehicles. The team focuses on creating UAVs that are better, cost-effective, and more efficient than the other UAVs available in the market. We take part in different national and international competitions to test our abilities and showcase our interests. Our motto is "To do the best we can do." With each competition that we take part in, we try to bring something new and groundbreaking into the world of aerial flight systems. There is genuine understanding as well as cooperation within the team, and at the same time, a sense of responsibility.

ACHIEVEMENTS

1. SAEINDIA AeroTHON 2023: The team participated in Phase 1 of the Design, Build and Fly Contest 2023. We managed to secure a rank of 48 out of the 84 participating teams!

2. DELIVERABLE 1: The team worked on the UAV challenge 2018 by Medical Express. The problem statement required to build an Autonomous Vertical Takeoff and Landing Aerial Vehicle system that can travel over a distance of 23 km, collect a blood sample and retrieve it back to the launching site. This challenge consists of three deliverables out of which Team Mavericks UAS cleared the first deliverable becoming one of the 55 international teams to do so.

Don't forget to check out our D1: here

3. OPERATION RAHAT: The team participated and completed the obstacle course of OP-RAHAT 2017 at IIT Bombay in 73 seconds.

4. FIRST AUTONOMOUS FLIGHT: We have had a Hexacopter that has the capability to autonomously land, takeoff, return to the launching site and travel through waypoints. We had successfully completed our first autonomous mission on February 15th 2018.
